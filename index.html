Family of Eateries

Restaurant App

HTML/CSS Brand Design UX/UI

Overview

In today's world, we experience almost everything through our devices, especially our mobile phones. We quickly adapted to integrating them into our daily lives due to their convenience and visually appealing displays. With just a tap, we can now access applications that seamlessly connect everything we need. Family of Eateries exemplifies this connectivity, efficiency, and user experience. Its intuitive interface enhances accessibility, allowing users to navigate effortlessly through the app’s key features: the main menu, nightly specials, and reservations.

Objective and Challenges

The objective of this project was to design a user-friendly mobile app for three restaurants within the same chain. In addition to developing the app, I was also responsible for creating the branding, including unique logos for each restaurant while maintaining a cohesive identity for Family of Eateries.

One of the key challenges was striking the right balance between individuality and unity in the branding. Each logo needed to stand out while still feeling connected to the overall chain. As for the mobile app, its functionality and visual presentation truly put my skills to the test. Researching how other mobile apps operate and how they showcase their offerings was essential in shaping the app’s accessibility and user flow.

Ensuring the app functioned seamlessly while delivering an intuitive UI was a top priority. Every element had to work harmoniously to create a smooth, engaging experience that captured the audience’s interest.

Deliverables

  • • Wireframes and prototypes
  • • Thoughtfully crafted visual designs, including layout, typography, and color scheme
  • • Custom-designed icons and logos
  • • Polished final product with seamless design, layout, and functionality

Branding

To create a strong visual connection between the restaurants, I designed each logo with a distinctive icon at its center, either replacing a letter or complementing it. This approach ensures each restaurant maintains its own unique identity while still feeling part of the larger brand family.

Fonts and Colours

I selected a modern and highly readable font to enhance clarity and user experience. The color scheme was designed to give each restaurant its own distinct personality while maintaining a cohesive brand identity. A unique color was assigned to each restaurant: blue, green, and red; creating a visually engaging and easily navigable interface. This thoughtful combination of typography and color ensures a seamless and aesthetically pleasing user experience.

Brand Design Marketing Materials Iconography

Overview

Nova Outdoors is designed for those who seek exploration, discovery, and the thrill of the unknown. Built for professionals and creatives aged 20 to 40, our brand embodies the spirit of adventure, empowering our community to embrace every journey. With durable, stylish, and functional outdoor gear. Nova Outdoors ensures reliability and individuality, whether navigating well-worn trails or forging new paths.

Objective and Challenges

The objective of this project was to establish a strong and recognizable brand identity for Nova Outdoors. This included designing a logo, defining a cohesive color palette, selecting typography, and ensuring these elements were applied consistently across marketing materials. The brand needed to reflect the adventurous and free-spirited nature of its audience while maintaining a sense of reliability, individuality, and effortless style.

One of the main challenges was capturing the uniqueness of Nova Outdoors and blending different design elements into a seamless and cohesive identity. The brand had to balance strength and approachability, ensuring it resonated with both seasoned outdoor enthusiasts and those new to exploration.

Another challenge was maintaining a strong connection to nature while ensuring the brand remained bold and contemporary. The visual identity had to feel organic yet structured, evoking trust while inspiring adventure. Careful consideration was given to how the colors, typography, and marketing assets worked together to tell a compelling story—one that encouraged exploration and reflected Nova Outdoors’ core values: trust, nature, and life.

Deliverables

  • • Logo design and refinement a nature-inspired emblem
  • • Thoughtfully crafted color palette and typography ensuring consistency and visual harmony
  • • Versatile branding assets strengthening brand presence across all platforms

Peru Museum

Data Driven Touchscreen App

UX/UI HTML/CSS JavaScript

Overview

This project is a data-driven touchscreen app designed to showcase the diverse cultures of Peru, including the Inca Empire, Chimu, and Nazca cultures. Built for museum visitors and history enthusiasts, the app offers an interactive experience with detailed drawings, informative content, and authentic imagery. Its goal is to educate and engage users, making history both accessible and immersive.

Objective and Challenges

The objective of this project was to create an interactive museum experience that brings Peru’s cultural heritage to life. It involved designing a touchscreen app featuring detailed information, original drawings, and images that support the historical content. The focus was on making the app visually captivating while ensuring it remained intuitive and easy for users to navigate.

One of the main challenges was presenting large amounts of historical information in a clear and engaging way. To make the experience unique, we created custom illustrations and digital paintings designed to look like historical pieces of art, adding authenticity and visual richness. The app’s design also had to strike a balance between being historically inspired and offering a smooth, modern user experience.

Deliverables

  • • Fully functional data-driven touchscreen app showcasing Peru’s diverse cultures
  • • Custom-designed illustrations and paintings styled to resemble historic artwork
  • • Intuitive user interface ensuring easy navigation and accessibility
  • • Authentic visual design inspired by Peru’s cultural heritage

Visual Design & Inspiration

The app’s design draws inspiration from Peru’s rich cultural history, incorporating earthy tones, textured backgrounds, and period-accurate iconography. Each culture is represented with its own distinct visual style, blending authentic patterns, colors, and motifs. The custom illustrations and digital paintings were crafted to resemble aged artifacts, adding a layer of realism and depth. The interface is both visually striking and highly user-friendly, creating an engaging and memorable museum experience.

Nova Outdoors: Retail Installation

Retail Product Display

Graphic Desing JavaScript HTML/CSS

Overview

This project is a retail installation designed for Nova Outdoors, showcasing an immersive brand experience. The installation features a interactive 360° rotating backpack display, an interactive digital catalog, and marketing materials accessible via QR codes. This dynamic setup enhances customer engagement, providing an interactive and informative experience.

Objective and Challenges

The objective of this project was to create an engaging and interactive retail experience for Nova Outdoors. The installation was designed to highlight key product features through a 360° rotating backpack experience, allowing customers to explore the design and functionality in detail. The interactive digital catalog provided additional product insights of the brand and allows the customer to purchase them trough a dual-screen display. To display more about the brand, marketing materials are accessable through QR codes.

One of the main challenges was ensuring that the installation effectively translate the brand elements and incorporate them seamlessly in each display. Each installation had different interactivity and had to be addressed differently and intuitively for the customer to easily travel within them.

Deliverables

  • • A interaactive 360° rotating backpack
  • • An interactive dual-screen catalog showcasing product details
  • • QR-accessible marketing materials for seamless customer interaction

360 Rotating App

The 360º rotating app delivers an immersive product experience using dynamic motion graphics and video. With hand sensor technology, users can explore Nova Outdoors’ featured product from every angle through natural gestures—no touch needed. A looping screensaver displays a brief product description and navigation bar, inviting users to interact seamlessly by hovering over the screen.

The platform serves as a dedicated space for sharing impactful stories through testimonies, photos, and videos, fostering cross-cultural connections and meaningful engagement.

Objective and Challenges

The main goal of this project was to design a website that effectively showcases the content created by the team, ensuring an engaging and accessible storytelling experience. The platform was developed to house various media assets, including testimonies, photographs, and videos, while maintaining a user-friendly and visually compelling design.

As the team leader, I was responsible for coordinating with both clients and team members, delegating tasks, and ensuring that client requirements were met in every meeting. I also facilitated discussions on content creation strategies to align with the project's vision.

One of the main challenges was ensuring that the website seamlessly integrated all media types while maintaining a cohesive and interactive user experience. Additionally, the asset creation process required careful planning to align with the storytelling goals of the FLCA project.

Deliverables
